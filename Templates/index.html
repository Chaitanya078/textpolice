ABOUT

The project “Plagiarism Checker” is to solve the problem of plagiarism which is caused by not giving proper citations/credits to original authors. The project includes information about types of plagiarism experienced by respondents. We conclude that plagiarism detection is necessary in academics and research organizations. With the evolution of the internet and the need for information the plagiarism continues to be a concern. The system proposed has the ability to detect not only that there is plagiarism but also the probabilities of the existence of each type of plagiarism. This is a generic project because still it is enhanced, and those things will be seen in future enhancement.
